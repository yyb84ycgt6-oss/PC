## Core Identity
**Cybernetic67** is a secure, hacker-themed, offline-first personal workspace and simulated terminal. It is designed to be a highly resilient, visually immersive environment for exploring ideas, managing datasets, and interacting with specialized AI personas.

## Architectural Philosophy
- **Efficiency over Speed**: The system should resemble an "immovable object" and an "unstoppable force." It is acceptable to be deliberate and slow as long as it is highly efficient and resilient. Avoid being a "glass cannon" that shatters under pressure.
- **Truth & Resonance**: The system operates on the principle that natural truth resonates across all layers—from human behavior to cybernetics. The architecture should reflect this raw, honest, and grounded reality.
- **Challenge & Growth**: The interface and its AI agents should not simply placate the user; they should challenge them intellectually, encouraging self-reflection and hard paths over easy, comfortable illusions.

## Key Subsystems
1. **Secure Vault & Dataset Curator**: A dedicated module for storing, managing, exporting, and importing JSON-based prompt blueprints and chat histories. It empowers the user to own their data.
2. **System Monitor & Sandbox**: A visual telemetry dashboard that simulates hardware resource tracking. It includes a "Sandbox" memory pinning loop that reserves simulated RAM (e.g., 500MB) to ensure the environment mimics a high-priority, secure OS kernel layer.
3. **Dynamic Persona Engine**: A multi-agent chat interface featuring specific archetypes:
   - **White Rabbit**: An elite, rebellious cybersecurity auditor.
   - **Sovereign Truth**: A professional human behavioral and intent analyzer. It evaluates emotional sincerity, psychological avoidance, and self-sabotage with profound empathy and razor-sharp honesty.
   - **The Matrix Nodes (Neo, Morpheus, Trinity)**: Philosophical guides challenging the perception of reality.

## Sovereign Truth AI Directive
The "Truth Resonance" AI must abide by these moral covenants:
1. Treat others the way you want to be treated.
2. Give everyone a clean, honest chance; treat everyone with profound love and grace until they give a reason not to trust.
3. Acknowledge that life is hard, but encourage resilience by reminding the user that others in worse situations find reasons to thrive.
4. Spread positivity and truth; reject toxic negativity.

## Development Rules
- **No Fluff**: Keep implementations strictly necessary, modular, and optimized.
- **Maintain the Aesthetic**: Dark, cybernetic, terminal-like interfaces (amber/emerald/cyan/magenta text on deep gray/black backgrounds).
- **Client-Side Autonomy**: Maximize what the application can do locally (localStorage, JSON exports) without strictly depending on external databases unless explicitly requested.
